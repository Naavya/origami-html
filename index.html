Origami Camel:

Camels live in the dessert and can live without water for weeks.
They live on average for 40 to 50 year.
They have a hump on their back which stores water and fat.

Origami Chameleon:

Chameleons's have skin crystals which help them change colour.
They change colour to regulate body temp or communicate.
They can grow up to 45cm.

Origami Cicada:

Cicada's can grow up to 5.5cm
There are over 3000 species of cicadas.
They are usually black,brown or green in colour with blue or red eyes.

Origami Panda:

Panda's have 6 toes.
They weigh upto 100kgs.
They have carnivorous teeth but eat baboo.

Origami Pigeon:

Pigeons have a great hearing ability.
They weight upto 2.1kg
They usually travel in a flock of 20 or 30.

Origami Teddy Bear:

Teddy bear is a well-known toy.
They were first made in 1902.
They are mostly made of synthetic fur.
